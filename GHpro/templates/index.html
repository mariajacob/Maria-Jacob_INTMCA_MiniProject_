About Palliative Care Mission

Panavhayathu's Palliative Care provides comprehensive support to individuals and families facing serious illnesses, prioritizing pain management, emotional well-being, and holistic care. Our mission focuses on maintaining dignity, respect, and comfort throughout their journey, empowering them with knowledge, resources, and compassionate assistance.
